AI in Fiction and Fact 2020

CSCI 4965 / COGS 4962

Course Description

The class will explore current AI topics through reading, writing, programming, and exploring some of the classic fiction that has former people's (mis)perceptions of machine intelligence. On one hand, machine intelligence provides cognitive assistance and complements humans to interpret data more efficiently and discover hidden knowledge in vast data resources. On the other hand, these algorithms may also affect the way humans perform some cognitive tasks and thus influence the autonomy and decision making of humans. This course will give an appreciation on how to weigh the two sides, separate fiction from fact, and how to critically evaluate the impact current and upcoming AI topics will have on society.
